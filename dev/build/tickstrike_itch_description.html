About the Game

Tickstrike is a tactical arena roguelite about finding the perfect moment to move and strike.

Enemies reveal where they will attack. Step out of danger, slip around their guard, and dash through them before they can surround you.

Clear each wave, choose a reward, and grow stronger throughout the run. Defeat all 10 demo waves—or continue fighting in Endless Mode.

Controls

  • Move: WASD
  • Aim: Mouse
  • Attack: Left Click
  • Dash: Hold Alt + Left Click
  • Wait: Space
  • Settings: Esc

How to Play

Every move, attack, dash, or wait advances the battle.

Highlighted tiles show where enemy attacks will land. Watch their countdowns and move away before they strike.

Enemies defend themselves from the front. Attack from the side or behind—or use your Dash—to break their Guard and stagger them.

Staggered enemies are vulnerable, so use that opening to finish them quickly.

Defeat every enemy to clear the wave and choose your next reward.
